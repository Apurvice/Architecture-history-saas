# Course Materials

This folder contains materials for the architecture history courses AR 103 (History of Indian Architecture) and AR 152 (History of Western Architecture).

## PDFs

- **indian-architecture/**: PDF documents related to Indian architecture history, including lecture notes, readings, and reference materials for AR 103.
- **western-architecture/**: PDF documents related to Western architecture history, including lecture notes, readings, and reference materials for AR 152.
- **comparative-studies/**: PDF documents that compare Indian and Western architecture, including analytical papers and study guides.

## Images

Images used in presentations, course materials, or for reference, such as architectural drawings, photographs, and diagrams.

## Presentations

Presentation files for lectures and course content, such as PowerPoint (.pptx), Keynote, or PDF slide decks.
